# Conceptual Roadmap Notes

This file can hold deeper roadmap thinking than the top-level `ROADMAP.md`.

The roadmap should be treated as a living orientation layer, not a schedule that forces Alpha into a predetermined form.

## Roadmap Questions

- What does Alpha need to protect before it can become powerful?
- Which concepts are stable enough to become specs?
- Which specs are ready to become protocols?
- Which protocols are ready to become technical tryouts?
- Which tryouts reveal something essential about Alpha rather than only showing a feature?
- Where does Alpha help shape life without making people feel managed?
- Where does Alpha need to stay quiet, wait, or do nothing?

## Roadmap Tension

Alpha needs enough structure to become real.

Alpha also needs enough openness to avoid becoming just another app, assistant, platform, or productivity tool.

The roadmap should hold that tension deliberately.
