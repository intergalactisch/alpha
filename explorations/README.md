# Explorations

Explorations are the concrete workings-out of Alpha.

This folder can hold technical tryouts, prototypes, implementation sketches, failure reports, diagrams, demos, and other material that helps Alpha become buildable.

The word matters: these are not final products. They are ways of working something out.

## Areas

- [Technical Tryouts](technical-tryouts/README.md): small experiments that test one idea, protocol, or primitive.
- [Main Implementation](main-implementation/README.md): space for a future integrated Alpha implementation.
- [Concept Artifacts](concept-artifacts/README.md): diagrams, mockups, generated forms, and visible conceptual outputs.

## Principle

Every exploration should make clear:

- what it is testing
- which Alpha concept it relates to
- what it assumes
- what it learned
- what failed or felt wrong
- what should change in the concept, spec, or protocol afterward
