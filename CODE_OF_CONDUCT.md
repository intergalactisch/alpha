# Code Of Conduct

Alpha should be shaped in a way that respects people.

This public repo should be welcoming to careful thinkers, builders, artists, researchers, and curious people who want to explore what Alpha could become.

## Expected

- Be respectful.
- Be specific when you disagree.
- Keep critique aimed at ideas, language, assumptions, or systems.
- Make room for uncertainty.
- Respect culture, art, memory, children, privacy, and personal boundaries.
- Assume the concept is evolving.

## Not Welcome

- harassment
- bullying
- hate
- threats
- manipulation
- spam
- impersonation
- exploitative behavior
- careless copying of creative work
- pressure to reveal private information

## Core Standard

Participation should help people shape life with care.
