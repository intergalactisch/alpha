# Public Repo Shape

This repository is the public working space for Alpha.

It is not only a documentation repo, and it is not yet a product repo. It is a place where concept, language, specs, protocols, research, tryouts, and implementation can slowly become legible to each other.

## Why Public

Alpha is about shaping life with care. If the concept is hidden, overly private, or only understandable to its creator, it becomes too easy for the idea to drift into abstraction without accountability.

Public visibility can help:

- make assumptions inspectable
- invite critique and contribution
- preserve the evolution of the idea
- keep security and trust central
- make the concept understandable before implementation hardens it
- let future builders understand why decisions were made

## What This Repo Can Hold

### Concepts

Conceptual writing explores what Alpha is, why it may deserve to exist, what old paradigms it responds to, and what new forms it could make possible.

### Ideology And Principles

Ideological documents state what Alpha should protect: agency, care, consent, memory, culture, creativity, children, trust, attention, and human connection.

### Specs

Specs describe Alpha-shaped systems with more precision. They are allowed to be incomplete, but should make their assumptions clear.

### Protocols

Protocols describe how people, agents, artifacts, memory, context, processes, and other parts can communicate.

Protocols should support transparency, consent, reversibility, and contextual nuance.

### Explorations

Explorations are concrete workings-out: experiments, prototypes, technical sketches, and implementation attempts.

They should be treated as learning material, not proof that Alpha has been fully defined.

### Changelog

The changelog records meaningful changes in the concept and repo structure.

### Roadmap

The roadmap shows direction without pretending the future is fixed.

## Repo Principle

The repository should be precise enough to build from, but open enough to remain alive.
