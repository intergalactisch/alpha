# Alpha Circles Product Sketch

Alpha Circles is one of the clearest current MVP probes for Alpha.

It is an AI social game master for real life: a system that turns a group intent into recurring missions, rituals, games, plans, memories, and artifacts.

It should not collapse the whole concept into social gaming. It is a compact testbed for the larger pattern: intent becomes experience, and fixed interfaces give way to adaptive systems.

## Product Promise

> For groups that want more than a group chat.

or:

> Alpha turns your group into a living world.

## Who It Is For

The first user is the initiator.

They are the person who says:

- "We should do something."
- "Why does nobody plan anymore?"
- "This Discord is getting quiet."
- "We need a better team ritual."
- "This birthday/weekend/trip could be special."

Alpha gives that person leverage without making them the unpaid manager of everyone else's social life.

## The First Magic Moment

The user writes:

> "We are six friends in our thirties. We used to hang out all the time, now we mostly send memes. I want us to feel like a crew again, but nothing too cringe."

Alpha returns:

- a group diagnosis
- a proposed four-week season
- a tone-calibrated theme
- first mission
- invite link
- message draft for the group
- shared mission page

The user should immediately feel:

> "Oh, this understands the social shape."

## V0 Flow

1. Create a Circle.
2. Describe the group and desired shift.
3. Choose tone and intensity.
4. Alpha proposes a season.
5. User edits or approves.
6. Alpha creates the first mission page.
7. User invites members.
8. Members respond asynchronously.
9. Alpha creates a recap artifact.
10. Alpha proposes the next mission.

## V0 Surfaces

### Organizer Console

Purpose:

- set intent
- approve missions
- adjust tone
- manage memory
- see participation

### Group Mission Page

Purpose:

- show the current mission
- collect responses
- let members react
- show deadline and stakes
- keep it lightweight

### Memory Timeline

Purpose:

- preserve highlights
- show recaps
- hold inside jokes
- track artifacts
- make continuity visible

### Season Page

Purpose:

- explain the arc
- show progress
- reveal upcoming beats lightly
- make the group feel part of something

## Season Types

### Reconnect

For friend groups that have gone quiet.

### Make Weekends Better

For people who want more intentional leisure.

### Creative Crew

For groups that want to make things together.

### Founder Circle

For small ambitious groups that want accountability without corporate vibes.

### Family Lore

For families that want traditions, memories, and low-friction activities.

### Community Quest

For Discords and creator communities.

## Design Taste

Alpha Circles should feel:

- private
- warm
- clever
- socially aware
- light enough to say yes to
- structured enough to create momentum

It should not feel:

- childish
- corporate
- like homework
- like therapy
- like fake engagement bait
- like a bot trying too hard

## The Wedge In One Sentence

Alpha Circles proves the big thesis by making one group experience one felt shift:

> "We did something together because Alpha made it easy, playful, and specific."
